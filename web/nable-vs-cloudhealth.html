nable vs CloudHealth

If you are a large enterprise standardizing governance across hundreds of accounts, CloudHealth is built for that. If you are an engineer or an AI-native team who wants answers and fixes without procurement, that is nable.

You are a large enterprise that needs org-wide governance, policy enforcement, chargeback and showback across many accounts and business units, with formal reporting. That scale of governance is what CloudHealth is for.

Common questions

Is nable a CloudHealth alternative?

For the engineer-facing cost use case, yes. nable answers cross-cloud cost questions and drafts fixes, self-serve and local. It is not an enterprise governance suite; CloudHealth is built for org-wide policy, chargeback, and reporting at large scale.

Do I need a sales call to use nable?

No. nable is self-serve: run uvx nable and you are querying cost in minutes. No procurement, no onboarding project.

Does nable do chargeback and showback?

nable attributes cost by team, service, and tag and exports reports, which covers many showback needs for smaller orgs. For enterprise-scale formal chargeback across hundreds of accounts, a governance platform like CloudHealth is purpose-built.

Where does nable run?

On your own machine. Credentials stay in your OS keychain and your bill is cached locally, unlike a hosted platform that ingests your billing data.
