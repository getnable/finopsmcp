Common questions

How much do LLM API tokens cost?

Pricing is quoted per million tokens, split into input (your prompt) and output (the completion), with output usually 3 to 5 times the input price. Small models run well under $1 per million input tokens; frontier models reach $15 or more. Your monthly cost is input tokens times input price, plus output tokens times output price, across the models you use.

How do I estimate my monthly OpenAI or Anthropic bill?

Multiply monthly input tokens by the input price per million, add output tokens times the output price per million. This calculator does it across popular models so you can compare. For your actual bill split by model, connect your usage with nable.

Does prompt caching reduce token cost?

Yes. Cached input tokens are billed at a steep discount, often around 90 percent off, by OpenAI and Anthropic. If much of your input is repeated context, caching cuts a real slice of the bill. Use the cache slider above to estimate it.

What is the cheapest model for my workload?

It depends on your input-to-output ratio. Output-heavy workloads punish models with expensive output; input-heavy workloads benefit most from caching. Enter your volumes and the table ranks models for your specific mix.
